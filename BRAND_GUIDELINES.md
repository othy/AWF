# AWF — Brand Guidelines

## 1. Plateforme de marque

Include:

* Nom complet
* Marque visible
* Descripteur

* Positionnement
* Promesse

* Vision

## 2. ADN de marque

Include:

* What AWF is
* What AWF is not
* Keywords
* Brand principles

## 3. Personnalité

Define AWF as:

* premium
* strategic
* direct
* modern
* rigorous
* creative
* business-oriented
* execution-focused

Also define what AWF must avoid:

* cheap agency tone
* generic community management positioning
* recruitment/staffing confusion
* empty buzzwords
* corporate coldness

## 4. Ton de voix

Define how AWF writes and speaks:

* clear
* expert
* confident
* structured
* result-oriented
* accessible to Moroccan decision-makers

Add examples:
Good:
“AWF construit un système marketing qui relie contenu, campagnes et conversion.”

Bad:
“Nous boostons votre marque avec des solutions innovantes et créatives.”

## 5. Naming rules

Explain how to use:

* AWF
* ALLO WORK FORCE
* AWF — Marketing Workforce

Add forbidden names:

* Allo Workforce
* Allo WorkForce
* AWF Agency
* AWF Recrutement
* Allo Work Force Agency

## 6. Logo direction

Describe the logo direction:

* premium typographic AWF mark
* modular system feeling
* no phone icon
* no headset
* no megaphone
* no rocket
* no generic social media icon

Include recommended logo versions:

* AWF
* AWF — Marketing Workforce
* ALLO WORK FORCE
* square icon/favicon

## 7. Color palette

Use this palette:

| Role             | Name           | Hex     |
| ---------------- | -------------- | ------- |
| Main background  | Obsidian Navy  | #070A12 |
| Surface          | Deep Graphite  | #0F172A |
| Elevated surface | Signal Card    | #151D2F |

* 70% dark/navy
* 20% white/grey
* 8% blue/cyan
* 2% amber

## 8. Typography

Define:

* Headings: Sora or Space Grotesk
* Body: Inter
* Labels, numbers and technical details: IBM Plex Mono or JetBrains Mono

Add usage rules:

* short headlines
* strong hierarchy
* generous spacing
* avoid long paragraphs
* use uppercase labels carefully

## 9. Layout system

Define the AWF layout style:

* 12-column grid on desktop
* generous spacing
* premium cards
* thin borders
* dark surfaces
* modular sections
* clear CTAs
* responsive-first design

## 10. Visual identity

Define the visual universe as:
“Marketing Control Room.”

Use:

* subtle grids
* modular cards
* dashboard-inspired visuals
* signal lines
* conversion pipelines
* dark premium interfaces
* glassmorphism used carefully
* performance metrics
* workflow visuals

Avoid:

* stock corporate photos
* cheap icons
* noisy gradients
* generic agency illustrations
* cliché digital marketing visuals

## 11. Motion principles

Define motion as:

* subtle
* useful
* premium
* fast
* non-intrusive

Use motion for:

* revealing systems
* showing flows
* guiding attention
* emphasizing conversion paths

Avoid:

* decorative animation
* heavy WebGL
* layout shift
* slow transitions
* excessive hover effects

## 12. Website rules

The website must communicate in 5 seconds:

1. AWF is a marketing company.
2. AWF is premium.
3. AWF executes, not only advises.
4. AWF connects content, campaigns and conversion.
5. AWF is built for Moroccan brands.

Every page must have:

* one clear goal
* one main CTA
* strong hierarchy
* clear business message
* clean SEO structure
* premium visual rhythm

## 13. Social media rules

AWF content must be:

* educational
* business-oriented
* practical
* visually premium
* clear and direct
* connected to Moroccan market realities

Recommended formats:

* carousels
* short Reels
* marketing breakdowns
* campaign audits
* before/after content
* tactical checklists
* case studies
* founder-led insights

Avoid:

* motivational filler
* generic marketing quotes
* overdesigned Canva templates
* irrelevant trends
* weak captions
* vague advice

## 14. Copywriting rules

AWF copy must connect:
problem → system → execution → result.

Recommended phrases:

* “Votre contenu doit travailler pour votre business.”
* “Une présence digitale sans système reste du bruit.”
* “AWF relie stratégie, contenu, campagnes et conversion.”
* “Le bon message, au bon format, amplifié au bon moment.”
* “Nous transformons l’attention en opportunités business.”

Forbidden phrases:

* “Boostez votre visibilité.”
* “Nous donnons vie à vos idées.”
* “Votre partenaire digital innovant.”
* “Des solutions créatives adaptées à vos besoins.”
* “Une communication 360° sur mesure.”

## 15. Brand applications

Define rules for:

### Website

Premium, dark, structured, conversion-first.

### Social media

Accessible, educational, sharp and visually consistent.

### Client proposals

Clear, business-oriented, structured by problem, solution, deliverables, timeline and pricing.

### Presentations

Minimal, premium, spacious, with strong titles and clear visual hierarchy.

### Ads

Short message, clear pain point, strong promise, direct CTA.

### Reports

Clean, factual, visual, KPI-focused.

## 16. Do & Don’t

Create a clear list of do and don’t examples.

Do:

* Use AWF as the main visible brand.
* Keep the design premium and structured.
* Speak about systems, execution and conversion.
* Use strong business language.
* Show methodology.
* Keep layouts clean.

Don’t:

* Make AWF look like a recruitment company.
* Use cheap agency clichés.
* Overuse icons.
* Use generic stock photos.
* Speak in buzzwords.
* Overdecorate.
* Promise vague results.
